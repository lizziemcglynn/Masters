About Me

My name is Lizzie McGlynn, and I am a graduate student in the Master of Technical and Professional Communication program at Auburn University. My academic and professional interests include technical editing, user-centered communication, digital content, professional writing, accessibility, and creative expression.

This portfolio features five projects from my graduate coursework. Some pieces reflect my independent work, while others showcase my ability to collaborate effectively in team-based settings. Together, these projects represent the range of rhetorical, analytical, editorial, and creative skills I have developed throughout the program.
